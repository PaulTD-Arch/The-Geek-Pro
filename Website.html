// About Me

More Than Blueprints.
I Build Solutions.

I'm a Civil Engineer based in Kampala with a focus on practical, ground-level infrastructure that creates measurable change for real communities.

"Growing up, I watched contaminated water affect children in our community. That moment shaped everything — I didn't just want to be an engineer. I wanted to be an engineer who fixes things that matter."

My approach is simple: understand the problem deeply before touching the tools. Whether it's a flooding neighbourhood, a crumbling road, or an unsafe construction site — I bring structured analysis and practical execution to every project.

Vision: To improve infrastructure quality and quality of life across Uganda, one well-engineered solution at a time.
